The Story of The Goodnews Centre (TGC)

RCCG - A Parish of the Redeemed Christian Church of God

The Redeemed Christian Church of God, The Goodnews Centre which is fondly known as TGC was planted on 12th of April 2009 and the venue for this grand take off was at #49 MCC Road which currently houses RCCG - House of David. God made this possible through Pastor Ola Adejubee as the Pastor in charge of Province being assisted by Late Pastor Inakhire Chamberlin of blessed memory during the season when Pst E. A. ADEBOYE instructed all provinces to create a Youth Church and we won't cease to mention that this was also facilitated by stOgaga Timeyin-Ekpo who was the Province Youth Pastor at the time, for Imo Province.

At the time of this planting, Bro. Paul Adepegba was chosen from Rhema Assembly Zone to pioneer this work. He was sent along with 12 other workers for this assignment.
