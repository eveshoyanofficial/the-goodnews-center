About RCCG TGC The Goodnews Centre

The Goodnews Centre (TGC) is a vibrant parish of the Redeemed Christian Church of God committed to spreading the gospel of Jesus Christ and building disciples for kingdom service.

Our mission is to:

  • Make heaven
  • Take as many people as possible with us
  • Have a member of RCCG in every family of all nations
  • Accomplish No. 1 above by living a holy life, and No. 2 and 3 above by propagating the gospel

We welcome you to join us for our worship services and experience the love of God in a warm and friendly atmosphere.
